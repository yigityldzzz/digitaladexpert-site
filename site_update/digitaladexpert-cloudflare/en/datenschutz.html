General Information

The following notes provide a simple overview of what happens to your personal data when you visit this website. Personal data is any data that can be used to personally identify you.

Data processing on this website is carried out by the website operator. You can find their contact details in the legal notice of this website.

How do we collect your data?

Your data is collected when you provide it to us (e.g., via the contact form). Other data is automatically collected by our IT systems when you visit the website (e.g., technical data such as internet browser, operating system, time of page access).

This website uses cookies only with your explicit consent. Cookies are small text files stored on your computer. They do not cause any damage and do not contain viruses. You can adjust your browser settings to reject cookies.

Cloudflare collects information that your browser transmits in server log files (e.g., browser type, date and time of access, IP address).
